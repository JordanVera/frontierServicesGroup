Who we are?

Founding partners Gregg Autrey and Dennet Vera of Houston founded Frontier Services Group LLC when in the early 2000’s they were asked to move and store contents for flood victims in Austin. Renting trucks, dryers and storage the principles for Frontier to ‘Restore Your Life Your Way’ was soon born.

more than a decade they witnessed residential and commercial disasters at their very worst…and people at their most devastated low points. Through their experiences of witnessing numerous people try to recover after terrible hardship and disaster, Gregg and Dennet realized that there were serious gaps that needed to be filled within the restoration industry.

Ultimately, because of their observations of what they thought was lacking in the industry, they created Frontier Services Group. Today Gregg and Dennet continue to go above and beyond to offer their customers an exceptional value and peace of mind on every project.

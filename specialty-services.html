Specialty Services

Specialty Biohazard Services

Frontier offers services used by companies or in special regrettable circumstances. We are discrete and understand when these problems arise and we get the job done quickly and efficiently.

We offer clean up services to commercial stores. When members of the public misuse public conveniences then Frontier is on call 24hours a day to clean up the bio-hazard mess. It’s a necessary service that has to be done to keep sensitive rooms sanitized and clean. As an independent crew we are able to keep shop staff away from contaminated areas which in turn insulates customers.

Unfortunately, deaths do occur on property. Sometimes its small animals that get trapped in a wall cavity or attic and can’t get out and sometimes, regrettably, people.

After the local officials, police or ambulance, release the site then Frontier is there to remove all contaminated surfaces from drywall to flooring and then clean sanitize everywhere. We bring a portable changing chamber that allows our crews to change into personal protective equipment and then to remove it and bag all PPE for bio-hazard disposal.

We follow bio-hazard protocol and any remaining tissue is transported in special designated double bagged boxes for incineration by specialty services.

History

Frontier has been in business since 2007. We first started as a 2 man, small sized company. Since then we have grown our employee rate to over 80 people. This is our history.



2007 - Frontier Opens Its Doors

Frontier starts up as a moving service with 2 employees, Dennet and Gregg. After being in the restoration business for some years prior to Frontier being born, Dennet and Gregg decided to fill in some of the holes in the restoration industry. Little did they know, the demand for a reputable and transparent natural disaster company was needed within the Houston market.

2008 - Hurricane Ike hits home

Hurricane Ike hits the Houston and Galveston areas hard, with an estimated damage cost of over $10 billion. As a company this was a defining point, it would either make us or break us. Frontier was able to scale during this time by perfecting the logistics behind each operation. Ultimately, Frontier serviced over 100 homes in our home town during this time period!

2011 - Bastrop wild fires

The Bastrop wild fires burned over 34,000 acres of land, and was the worst recorded wildfire in the state of Texas. It torched 1,660 homes, killed two people and injured 12 others. Frontier is proud to have serviced native Texans throughout this tough time.

2013 - More Office space

In 2013 Frontier Services Group was fortunate enough to upgrade it's office space by leasing an extra unit in our office space. We went from 3,000sqft to 7,000sqft, enabling FSG to scale it's operation in ways we didn't even know!

2017 - Hurricane Harvey Destroys Houston

It's no stranger that Houston is fond of hurricanes, but when Harvey hit it was total devestation. Harvey was the greatest hurricane to hit our state since the Great Galveston Hurricane. Totaling a whopping $125 billion in damages and affecting over 135,000 homes, Harvey devasted our city like we've never seen before. As soon as the water from the streets dried up Frontier was at work, working 24/7 to help people restore their houses back to living conditions.

2018 - New Office Space

Thanks to the success of our company, FSG was able to upgrade its headquarters to a new, 24,000sqft office/warehouse space. It was truly a dream come true. To think that 2 guys in a truck could make it into a warehouse space that big was amazing, and our biggest accomplishment yet!

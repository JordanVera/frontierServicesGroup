Disaster Recovery

Description

When your home is flooded from rising water then your homeowner’s coverage won’t help you. That’s because homeowner insurance only covers losses that arise from inside the physical structure of your home or covered outbuildings.


Only a FEMA flood policy, which you can buy through any insurance agent, provides flood coverage. But adjusters can be a days, weeks or months and getting you a payment can sometimes take over a year. To save your house as soon as the flood waters reside you need to remove carpets, wood trim, drywall and insulation from all affected walls at least 12” above the high water mark and then all affected bathroom and kitchen cabinets and appliances.


Harvey flooding was to roof tops of some homes and was often to four feet or more. That’s major flooding! Many homeowners just did not know what to do and today their homes are a mass of seething mold making the homes an unsalable liability. The State of Texas can step in where mold is 25 continuous square feet or more and take action.


Frontier can help, but, the longer you leave a home the worse the mold and our Disaster Recovery Service (DRS) will cost more because there's more work to do. Whether or not you have FEMA coverage Frontier will be ready to help everyone as we only work on a cash pay basis for Emergency Disaster Recovery work.


First Come First Served

If you call Frontier as soon as your home floods from a Disaster you can be placed on our First Come First Serviced list. As soon as the flood water recedes not only in your home, but from roads needed to get to your home than we set and appointment to see you. Remember, even Frontier has limited resources of skilled technicians and equipment. So get on the list as soon as you know you have a flood problem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our disaster recovery branch is set up to respond to just that, natural disasters. A traditional water loss comes from inside the home, sources include pipe burts, AC leaks, etc. Disaster recovery losses occur when water invades your home from outside the home in a flood or roof leak from a rain storm.

Costs vary by location, access and time. Our cost is based on the area of your home plus half the area of your garage (assuming only walls are affected). Frontier will endeavor to keep the cost as low as possible. We can offer to customize any service you require to suit your needs and your budget.

If you have FEMA, your coverage can supply you with a Photo Report and summary of work we performed and our charges so you can claim for reimbursement. For Emergency Disaster Recovery all work is undertaken on a cash only basis with half up front before we start and balance on completion.

Repairing your home once mitigation has dried out the home and removed any affected materials is a separate process and separate estimate. Some larger companies, like Frontier Services Group, have their own in-house reconstruction departments. However, you can shop around and decide to be your own project manager for rebuilding.

Water Removal

Description

Serving Houston, TX and surrounding areas - Water damage can be devastating to all aspects of your home. The Frontier Services Group, LLC will work with you to ensure water damage repair is handled appropriately to avoid mold and much more. We're able to provide you with help where and when you need it through our 24/7 emergency service. Water damage can leave devastating results in your home or business. Whether you’re dealing with water damage from a flood, fire, broken pipes, sewer backups, water leaks from a bathtub, toilet or sink, it’s important that you find someone that will not only remove standing water in your home, but is experienced in water damage restoration. Even when the water is gone, without proper restoration services, the damage will remain. When you need a reliable company to bring your home or business back to its original state, call Frontier Services Group in Houston.


Frontier Services Group is here to help so you don’t have worry about water cleanup on your own. Thoroughly removing all standing water is stressful, time consuming and best left to a professional company. And once the water is gone, the last thing you want to do is escalate the stress by trying and failing to restore your property on your own. The professionals at Frontier Services Group continue to be the top choice for water damage restoration in Houston and the surrounding areas because we are committed to providing quality restoration services. We are available 24/7 for emergency water removal and water extraction. We handle every job promptly and efficiently, so you can get on with normal activates at home or work. At Frontier Services Group, we get the job done right the first time. With our help, you won’t have to worry about lasting water damage to your property.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have a claim number then they will just need a few signatures on forms allowing them to start work and work with your insurance carrier.Frontier will remove cabinets with your adjuster’s approval. We will also get moisture readings and infrared pictures to show your adjuster and discuss the best option for your home and the damage.

Typically, water losses confined to one or two rooms should take 3 to 5 days. Whole house damage from deep flooding will take longer as there’s more demolition and work needed.

Carriers differ on deductibles. Many insurance companies will apply your deductible against reconstruction and pay the mitigation contractors direct first. However, it also depends on the amount of the loss as well as the carrier’s policy and sometimes on your mortgage company.

Repairing your home once mitigation has dried out the home and removed any affected materials is a separate process and separate estimate. Some larger companies, like Frontier Services Group, have their own in-house reconstruction departments. However, you can shop around and decide to be your own project manager for rebuilding.
